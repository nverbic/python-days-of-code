ittehgapS alla aranobrac, a lacipyt namoR hsid
emoR's enisiuc sah devlove hguorht seirutnec dna sdoirep fo laicos, larutluc, dna lacitilop segnahc. emoR emaceb a rojam lacimonortsag ertnec gnirud eht tneicna ega. tneicnA namoR enisiuc saw ylhgih decneulfni yb tneicnA keerG erutluc, dna retfa, eht eripme's suomrone noisnapxe desopxe snamoR ot ynam wen, laicnivorp yraniluc stibah dna gnikooc seuqinhcet.[881]

retaL, gnirud eht ecnassianeR, emoR emaceb llew nwonk sa a ertnec fo hgih-enisiuc, ecnis emos fo eht tseb sfehc fo eht emit dekrow rof eht sepop. nA elpmaxe fo siht saw oemolotraB ippacS, ohw saw a fehc gnikrow rof suiP VI; eh deriuqca emaf ni 0751 nehw sih koobkooc arepO lled'etra led eranicuc saw dehsilbup. nI eht koob eh stsil yletamixorppa 1,000 sepicer fo eht ecnassianeR enisiuc dna sebircsed gnikooc seuqinhcet dna sloot, gnivig eht tsrif nwonk erutcip fo a krof.[981]


aicnoC id enihccuz, na elpmaxe fo namoR-hsiweJ enisiuc
ehT oiccatseT, emoR's edart dna esuohrethguals aera, saw netfo nwonk sa eht "ylleb" ro "esuohrethguals" fo emoR, dna saw detibahni yb srehctub, ro iraniccav.[091] ehT tsom nommoc ro tneicna namoR enisiuc dedulcni eht "htfif retrauq".[091] ehT dlo-denoihsaf adoc alla araniccav (liatxo dekooc ni eht yaw fo srehctub)[091] si llits eno fo eht ytic's tsom ralupop slaem dna si trap fo tsom fo emoR's stnaruatser' sunem. bmaL si osla a yrev ralupop trap fo namoR enisiuc, dna si netfo detsaor htiw secips dna sbreh.[091]

nI eht nredom ega, eht ytic depoleved sti nwo railucep enisiuc, desab no stcudorp fo eht ybraen angapmaC, sa bmal dna selbategev (ebolg sekohcitra era nommoc).[191] nI lellarap, namoR sweJ – tneserp ni eht ytic ecnis eht ts1 yrutnec CB – depoleved rieht nwo enisiuc, eht anicuc ociaduig-acsenamor.

selpmaxE fo namoR sehsid edulcni accobmitlas alla anamor – a laev teltuc, namoR-elyts, deppot htiw war mah dna egas dna deremmis htiw etihw eniw dna rettub; ifoicrac alla anamor – sekohcitra namoR-elyts, retuo sevael devomer, deffuts htiw tnim, cilrag, sbmurcdaerb dna desiarb; ifoicrac alla aiduig – sekohcitra deirf ni evilo lio, lacipyt fo namoR hsiweJ gnikooc, retuo sevael devomer, deffuts htiw tnim, cilrag, sbmurcdaerb dna desiarb; ittehgaps alla aranobrac – ittehgaps htiw nocab, sgge dna onirocep; dna ihccong id onilomes alla anamor – anilomes gnilpmud, namoR-elyts.[291]
